Few facts about the world we live in.

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and is thelargest of all the terrestrial planets of the solar system. The Earth is the only planet in our solar system not to be named after a Greek or Roman deity. The Earth was formed approximately 4.54 billion years ago and is the only known planet to support life.

About the Earth

  • Mass: 5.972 x 1024 kg
  • Equatorial diameter: 12.756 x 106 m
  • Notable moons: Moon

Top 3 facts about the Earth:

  1. The Earth's rotation is gradually slowing down.
  2. Earth is believed to be at the centre of the universe.
  3. Earth has a powerful magnetic field.
